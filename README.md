## 🏛️ Comprehensive Operational Framework Index

### 📋 Phase 0: Procurement & Sizing Gates
*   **Corm Sizing Brackets:** Strict physical sorting utilizing 9-11 cm and Premium 11+ cm circumference benchmarks.
*   **Asset Counting Densities:** Mathematical calculation models for loading capacities per square foot of rack footprint.
*   **Supplier Sample Auditing:** Visual verification indicators for structural fungal and physical integrity before facility onboarding.

### 🧼 Phase 1: Biosecurity & Sanitation
*   **Conventional Sanitation Track:** 10% Sodium Hypochlorite (Bleach) wet dip matrix with mandatory fresh RO water rinsing.
*   **Certified Organic Sanitation Track:** Pure cold-pressed Neem Oil botanical emulsion combined with liquid Castile soap.
*   **Drying Protocols:** The 20-minute field-level flash dry framework to prevent early moisture trapping.

### 🌡️ Phase 2: Sprouting & Flowering (Room A)
*   **The Sprouting Window:** 15–21 days of Total Darkness (0 Hours ON / 24 Hours OFF) at 16°C–18°C Day and 13°C–15°C Night profiles to force vertical sprout elongation.
*   **The Blooming "Cold Shock" Trigger:** 15–20 days of Full-Spectrum LED lighting (10 Hours ON / 14 Hours OFF) at 100–150 µmol/m²/s paired with an intense 8°C–10°C nighttime thermal drop to trigger synchronous blooming.
*   **Biosecurity Auditing:** Standardized workflows for **The Smell Test, The Eye Test, and The Touch Test** to isolate and cull latent fungal outbreaks.

### 🌸 Phase 2.1 & 2.2: Post-Harvest Processing & Storage
*   **The Morning Pluck SOP:** Strict "Closed Bud" harvesting windows to eliminate sticky yellow pollen contamination on red stigmas.
*   **Precision Dehydration:** Dehydrator matrix rules holding 45°C to 50°C for 2 to 3 hours to lock in premium Crocin color and Safranal aroma ratings.
*   **Secondary Revenue Biomass Upcycling:** Sourcing, drying, and packing protocols for **Purple Petals** and **Yellow Stamens** for cosmetic B2B supply lines.
*   **Zero-Cost Home Storage Chemistry:** Micro-environment stabilization keeping moisture **strictly under 40% RH** utilizing food-grade 2g silica gel packets inside airtight amber glass jars.

### 🌱 Phase 3: Sowing, Vegetative Growth, & Nutrition (Room B)
*   **Substrate Conditioning Protocol:** 12–16 hour overnight cocopeat hydration and ionic calcium buffering at an acidic **pH of 5.5 to 6.2** using Citric Acid.
*   **Multi-Track NPK Nutrition Matrix:** Scaled 100L recipes delivering a low-nitrogen, high-potassium **2:1:4 proportional weight ratio** (40g N / 20g P / 80g K) with automated morning micro-overhead sprinkler automation.
*   **The 14-Day Starter Protocol:** Diluted half-strength fertigation routines at **0.8 mS/cm EC** to nourish emergent root tips without chemical burning.

### 📦 Phase 4: Corm Extraction & Sizing Grade Gate
*   **The Curing Drought Trigger:** Complete 10–14 day automated irrigation shut-off to harden and cure new daughter corm skins inside bone-dry beds.
*   **The 8-Gram Grading Gate:** Sorting tracking separating small replication corms (<8g) back into Room B's loop from premium flowering corms (>8g) heading into summer thermal incubation.
